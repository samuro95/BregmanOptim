BurgEntropy

class deepinv.optim.bregman.BurgEntropy[source]

Bases: Bregman

Module for the using Burg’s entropy as Bregman potential \(\phi(x) = - \sum_i \log x_i\). The corresponding Bregman divergence is the Itakura-Saito distance \(D(x,y) = \sum_i x_i / y_i - \log(x_i / y_i) - 1\). As shown in https://publications.ut-capitole.fr/id/eprint/25852/1/25852.pdf, it is the Bregman potential to use for performing mirror descent on the Poisson likelihood deepinv.optim.data_fidelity.PoissonLikelihood.
